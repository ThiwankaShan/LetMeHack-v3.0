LETME HACK v3.0

LetMeHack v3.0 is centred around generative artificial intelligence (AI). This first-ever product-based generative AI Hackathon in Sri Lanka aims to bring together a diverse group of participants from all the universities and higher educational institutes of Sri Lanka to collaborate, innovate, and explore the vast potential of generative AI technology, which is an emerging and relevant field that can inspire and empower the next generation of creators and innovators.

hackathon offers an ideal platform for participants to develop cutting-edge solutions and prototypes while fostering creativity and pushing the boundaries of AI.

Moreover, we are committed to organizing our hackathon this year as well in an environmentally friendly way, as we believe that technology and the environment can work together.
